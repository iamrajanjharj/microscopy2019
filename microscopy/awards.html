Young Scientist Award

The award shall be presented to the researcher under the age of 40 years for his/her credentials and quality of research presentation. A medal and Certificate of Merit shall be awarded to the winner(s). To apply mention “Award Category” during Abstract/Paper submission. Please note that the same abstract shall not be considered under the more than one award category.

Best Paper Presentation Award

The award shall be presented for the best paper(s) presentation during the conference. An award memento and Certificate of Merit shall be awarded to the winner(s). To apply mention “Award Category” during Abstract/Paper submission. Please note that the same abstract shall not be considered under the more than one award category.

Best Microscopy Picture Award

The award shall be presented for the best microscopy picture submitted under the competitive category. An award memento and Certificate of Merit shall be awarded to the winner(s). To apply mention “Award Category” during Abstract/Paper submission. Please note that the same abstract/picture shall not be considered under the more than one award category.

Grants

Limited number of discounted Registrations, Accommodations and Travel Grants are available. The candidates should apply with proper justification and endorsement from their Head of the Institution.

Best Foldscope Microscopy Award

The award shall be presented for the best foldscope microscopic data and pictures submitted under the competitive category. An award Memento and Certificate of Merit shall be awarded to the winner(s). To apply mention “Award Category” during abstract/Paper submission. Please note that the same abstract/picture shall not be considered under the more than one award category.
